Deep Fake Audio Detection and Generation

This project uses machine learning to detect and create deep fake audio. It can identify fake voice recordings and also generate realistic AI voices. The goal is to make the system accurate and reliable.

Main Features:

  • Detect Fake Audio: Finds and flags manipulated voice clips.
  • Generate AI Voices: Creates human-like synthetic voices.
  • Improve Accuracy: Uses advanced models for better results.
  • Fast and Efficient: Processes audio quickly and effectively.

This system can be useful for media verification, security, and AI voice applications, helping to prevent fake audio misuse.
